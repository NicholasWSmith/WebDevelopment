Nicholas Smith's Orange Soya Sauce Steak


The end result for the Soya Sauce Steak
Prep Time: 8-24 Hours (Depends on time for Marinade)
Cook Time: 8-16 Minutes (Depending on desired meat)
Prepares 2 lb of boneless steak (6 servings)
You can download the recipe here!

Ingredients:
  • 1/2 Cup Soy Sauce
  • 1/4 Cup White Vinegar
  • 1/4 Cup Orange Juice
  • 2 Tbsp orange marmalade
  • 1 Clove Garlic
  • 1/4 Tsp Chili Powder

Instructions:
  1. Mix ingredients in a non-metallic shallow dish
  2. Dry off beef (helps the marinade)
  3. Add beef to non-metallic dish
  4. Turn to coat
  5. Cover and refrigerate for 8-24 hours, turning occasionally
  6. Remove steak from marinade, reserving marinade
  7. Place on greased grill over medium hot coals
  8. Cook, turning and brushing occasionally with marinade
  9. Cook for 10 minutes per side or until medium-rare
  10. Enjoy!
