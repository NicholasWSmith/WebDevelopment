EXPLANATION OF THIS BUILD

Hey everyone, I know that a lot of people have been saying that Exploding Palm isn't that useful anymore, but I frankly disagree for lower level Torments. For example, in Torment levels 1-4 (Aprox), an average minion has about 5-20 million HP. With the old Exploding Palm, it would do around 2.5-10 mil damage when it died. With the NEW exploding palm and my current gear, it does about 60 million damage in crits. Which is fanastic. So if you are attacking a Rare/Elite/Boss type, and there are minions/monsters around you. Target THEM. Pull them in, use exploding palm on them and kill them. This will do around 60 million damage to everything in that area, and if you have 2 or 3 exploding palms that is about 120-180 million damage. The elite mod will DIE. As you can see in the video to the right, I do about 11mil effective DPS. The elite mobs died VERY quickly, and they had about 300 million health a peice.
